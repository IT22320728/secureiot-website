Simple Journey

How it works

Customers understand solutions faster when the workflow is simple and direct.

1

Connect your IoT devices

Smart devices join the network through a controlled gateway environment.

2

Verify device identity

Certificate-based identification and Zero Trust checks ensure only trusted devices are granted proper access.

3

Monitor and detect anomalies

The platform observes traffic flow, trust scores, and behavioural indicators to detect suspicious activities.

4

Enforce adaptive security policies

The SDN controller updates rules dynamically to allow, block, restrict, or isolate risky behaviour.

5

Trap attackers and learn from threats

Malicious traffic can be redirected to honeypots where attacks are studied for improved future defense.
