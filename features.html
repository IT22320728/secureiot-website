Product Value

Why choose SecureIoT-SDN?

This section presents your project like a real product customers can trust and adopt.

🛡️

Real-Time Protection

Continuously monitors network traffic and device behaviour to identify threats before they spread across your IoT environment.

🤖

ML-Based Threat Detection

Uses machine learning techniques to analyze network traffic, identify unusual behaviour, and support early detection of potential cyber threats in IoT environments.

🔐

Zero Trust Security

Every device must be identified, verified, and continuously evaluated before it is granted access to sensitive network resources.

🌐

SDN Policy Control

Software-defined networking allows adaptive enforcement policies such as allow, restrict, quarantine, or redirect in real time.

🕵️

Honeypot-Based Defense

Suspicious attackers can be diverted into controlled honeypot environments to collect threat intelligence and reduce risk to real devices.

💡

Low-Cost Deployment

Built with lightweight technologies like Raspberry Pi, ESP32, Docker, and open-source tools, making it suitable for SOHO use cases.
